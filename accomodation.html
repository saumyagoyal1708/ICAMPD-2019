Accommodation

Accommodation will be arranged in KIIT Hostels as per the availability on payment basis (@Rs500 per day per head) for which the organizers will provide necessary assistance if informed in advance with travel plan. Accommodations provided will be AC room with attached washroom, which will be shared by two persons. Near around KIIT many guest houses and hotels are also available which can be booked using different apps for hotels.
