Publications:

All successfully reviewed and accepted papers presented at ICAMPD-2019 will be published in Sci Index Journals/Scopus indexed proceedings. Best posters from TechExpo will also get a chance to publish full paper in Scopus indexed proceedings.

Submission:

Prospective authors of contributory papers are requested to submit their full manuscript limited to a maximum of 10 pages to:

https://easychair.org/cfp/ICAMPD219

The authors interested ICAMPD-2019 Tech-Expo for are requested to submit an extended abstract (within 500 words) related to theme of the conference through email (techexpo.icampd@kiit.ac.in). In case of any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us.
