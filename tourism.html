Bhubaneshwar "Temple City of India"

With the Architectural legacy of the kalinga dynasty bhubaneshwar boasts with the hundreds of temples Lingraj , Mukteswar , RajaRani , Rameshwar , Vital etc. Dating from 6-13th century AD . Apart from remarkably sculptured temples, the jain caves of Khandagiri and Udhaygiri , Nadankanan Zoological park , The Piece Pagoda "Dhauli" on the bank of the river Gaya attracts visitor from all over the world. Besides , Puri , Konark along with capital city of Bhubaneshwar form the three vertices of Golden triangle of Orrisa tourism circuit.
